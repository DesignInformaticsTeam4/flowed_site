Why Travel Actively?

It's good for you.
Active travel encourages increased physical activity, reducing obeasity and improving general well-being.

It's good for the environment.
over 50% of car-trips in the U.K. are less than five kilometers: short distances that lend can be replaced by active travel. By walking we cut carbon emissions and improve air-quality.

It's good for business.
By encouraging people to explore places off the main thoroughfare, we expose them to businesses they otherwise wouldn't have encountered.

More Than Monuments

Explore the city through it's stories.

Taking active design into consideration when designing new developments can help increase participation in active travel. However, how do we encourage active travel within our existing communities; how do we encourage active travel in areas where we can't change the physical layout?

When surveying pedestrians, we found many used inefficient routes to get through the city. Many of these paths were along main roads with high-density traffic; roads with poorer air-quality and more sound-polution.

FlowED aims to introduce people to lesser-known parts of the city through a city-wide scavenger hunt. Through games we can encourage people to use the city more effectively.
